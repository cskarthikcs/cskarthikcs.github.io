Karthik C. S.

Sometimes, I scribble my thoughts.
Below is a selected collection in reverse chronological order.


  • The room was dark.
    As I was lighting a candle,
    I burnt my finger.
    I threw the matchstick to the ground,
    And cursed into the air.
    The room was dark again,
    And it dawned on me-
    The fire encroaching the matchstick
    Did not discriminate between wood and flesh;
    And I was happy with the light it shed in the room,
    Until it burnt my finger, and caused me pain.
    I was a leech.
    The fire was only carrying out its duty.


  • The chicken decided to cross the road,
    To find out if life was better on the other side.
    A truck hit the chicken while it was crossing,
    And it found out that the other side was simply,
    An eternal blank.


  • Dominoes on the wall,
    They see it all;
    As we brawl,
    As we crawl,
    And finally,
    As we fall,
    They see it all,
    They know it all.


  • ನಿದ್ರೆಯ ಲೋಕದಲ್ಲಿ ತೇಲುತ್ತಿರುವ ಚೆಲುವೆ,
    ನಿನ್ನ ಪಾದ-ಕುಸುಮವು, ಎದರಿಗಿರುವ ನನಗೊಮ್ಮೆ ತಾಕಲಿ.
    ಪರಿಮಳದ ಅಂದ-ಗಂಧದಿಂದ ಕೆಣಕುವೆ, ಇದು ಸರಿಯೇ?
    ಸರಿ ಇದ್ದರು, ಇರದಿದ್ದರೂ, ನೆನಪಿನ ಹಿತ ನನಗಿರಲಿ.


  • Strong harsh winds might blow;
    Stay strong, and don't let the fire go.
    But once the struggle has ended;
    Wipe away the tears expended,
    To continue the one true fight,
    Of spreading warm kindly light.


  • Words may fail,
    But actions survive, so-
    Breathe into the space,
    And you shall surface.


  • ಅರಳುವ ಬೊಜ್ಜಿನ
    ಕಾರಣ ಅತಿ ಮಧುರ


  • He did not know much about her,
    And yet he moved closer.
    The closer he got,
    The more he thought-
    Of what he would say;
    And what if she said nay.
    That broke his courage,
    And he forever shall never know,
    Of how she would say nay,
    Because she way gay.


  • ಸನಿಹ ಸನಿಹದಲೆ,
    ಒಲವ ಮಿಡಿವ ಅಲೆ,
    ಚಿಮ್ಮಿ ಹೊಮ್ಮುತಲೆ,
    ನೀ ಬಂದೆ ಸನಿಹದಲೆ.


  • If roses could tell,
    A story a day:
    On one fine morning,
    In the midst of May;
    I saw you,
    From far away;
    I will leave the rest,
    For the roses to say;
    Of how I met you,
    And our love-
    Is here to stay.


  • Of swiftful strides,
    And moonlit smiles,
    She walks past;
    I catch her on my right,
    And move with her to my left,
    She's now in my past.


  • ಎಂದೋ ಮರೆತ ಹಾಡನ್ನು,
    ನೆನಪಿಸಿದೆ ನೀನು.
    ನೆನಪಿಸಿದ ಹಾಡನ್ನು,
    ಹಾಡಲು ಬಳಿ ಬಂದರೆ-
    ಮರಳಿ ನೆನಪಿಸಿದೆ,
    ಚಪ್ಪಲಿ ಏಟನ್ನು.


  • ಎಂದೋ ಮರೆತ ಹಾಡನ್ನು,
    ನೆನಪಿಸಿದೆ ನೀನು.
    ನೆನಪಿಸಿದ ಹಾಡನ್ನು,
    ಹಾಡಲು ಬಳಿ ಬಂದರೆ-
    ಹೇಳಿದೆ ನೀನು,
    'ನಾನು ಅವಳಲ್ಲಾ ಅವನು'.
