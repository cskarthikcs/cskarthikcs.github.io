Seminar on Additive Combinatorics


Sundays: 11:15-13:15
Lecture Room 208, Math & CS Building II

In the first part (i.e., the first six lectures) of the seminar, we will follow (parts of) the survey by Lovett.
From then on, each lecture of the seminar will be the presentation of a research article or an uncovered topic from a survey (on additive combinatorics) by a seminar participant.
